(There may also be a confirmation link in that email — click it if there is,

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER THAT
-----------------------
Whenever someone fills in the form on your site, you'll get an email straight
to yshandymen@gmail.com with their name, phone number, email, the type of job
they picked, and their description of the problem. You just reply or call them
back.

You can reply to the email directly and it goes to the customer.


COMMON QUESTIONS
----------------
Is it really free?
  Yes. The free plan covers 250 enquiries per month. You'd have to be getting
  8+ enquiries every single day to go past that.

Do I need to log in anywhere or remember a password?
  No. There's no dashboard to manage and nothing to maintain. The key just
  tells the form where to send the emails.

Is it safe to put that code on the website?
  Yes. It's designed to be public — it only lets the form send email TO you.
  Nobody can use it to read your email or send email as you.

What if I don't want to do this?
  The form still works without it — it just opens the visitor's own email app
  with the details filled in, and they press send. It works, but some people
  drop off at that step, so the setup above will get you more enquiries.

Will I get spam?
  The form has a hidden anti-spam trap built in that blocks automated bots.
