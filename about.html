About Y.S. Handymen

Quality work. Reliable service. Peace of mind. Three things that sound obvious — and are exactly what people tell us is hardest to find.

Who We Are

Run by Yosef Silberstein

Y.S. Handymen LLC is a handyman service working with homeowners across Cleveland. When you call, you speak to the person who's going to do the work — not a call centre, and not a rotating cast of subcontractors.

Most of what we handle falls into the awkward middle: too small for a contractor to bother with, too fiddly or time-consuming to keep putting off yourself. A faucet that's been dripping for months. A door that never latched properly. A disposal making a noise you've learned to ignore.

Individually they're minor. Together they're the difference between a house that feels looked after and one that feels like it's slowly getting away from you. That's the gap we fill — and why the tagline is small jobs, big difference.
