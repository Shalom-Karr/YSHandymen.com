What We Do

Repairs, replacements
& everything on the list

Handyman work covers a lot of ground. Here's where most of our calls land — if what you need isn't listed, ask anyway.

01

Plumbing Repairs

Drips, leaks and slow drains — caught early before they turn into water damage and higher bills.

  • Leaking faucets & hose bibs
  • Running or leaking toilets
  • Clogged and slow drains
  • Under-sink and supply-line leaks
02

Fixture Replacements

Old fixture past saving? We pull the tired one and set the new one properly — sealed, level and leak-free.

  • Faucets & shower heads
  • Toilets & sinks
  • Light fixtures & ceiling fans
  • Cabinet handles & hardware
03

Garbage Disposals

From clogs to leaks, we get your disposal working like new — or swap it out if it's reached the end.

  • Disposal not turning on
  • Jams & clogs
  • Leaking disposals
  • Strange noises & full replacement
04

Walls & Doors

The small damage that makes a room feel neglected — patched, sanded and sorted.

  • Drywall holes & cracks
  • Doors that stick or won't latch
  • Locks, handles & hinges
  • Trim, moulding & caulking
05

Mounting & Assembly

Anchored into studs and set straight — the things that need to hold weight and stay put.

  • TV wall mounting
  • Shelving & brackets
  • Mirrors, blinds & artwork
  • Flat-pack furniture assembly
06

General Handyman Work

The running list of little things. Give us the whole list and we'll work through it in one visit.
